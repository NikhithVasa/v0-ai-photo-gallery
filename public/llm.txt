# SaathiDesk LLM Guide

Last updated: 2026-06-14

SaathiDesk is a private AI-powered photo gallery platform for wedding and event
photography. The product combines client galleries, people filters, semantic
photo search, AI photo review, AI image edits, LUT presets, collage exports,
private sharing, passcodes, and watermark/download controls.

## Primary Pages

- `/` - Public homepage with documentation, AI workflow, prompt guidance, and
  product walkthroughs.
- `/login` - Sign in and sign up.
- `/albums` - Authenticated album list.
- `/albums/new` - Create a new album.
- `/albums/[albumSlug]` - Main album gallery with Photos and People views.
- `/albums/[albumSlug]/upload` - Upload photos into an existing album event.
- `/albums/[albumSlug]/events/new` - Add or manage an album event.
- `/albums/[albumSlug]/culling` - AI review and culling workspace.
- `/albums/[albumSlug]/collage` - Album-scoped collage builder.
- `/collage` - General collage entry point.
- `/customers` - Customer management for studios.
- `/customers/[customerSlug]` - Customer-scoped albums and access.
- `/presets` - Public preset marketplace.
- `/presets/my` - Saved and uploaded presets.
- `/presets/upload` - Upload a `.cube` LUT preset with preview images.
- `/share/[token]` - Public share-link album access.
- `/legal/privacy-policy` and `/legal/terms-of-service` - Legal pages.

## What The AI Does

AI processing is scoped to an album and event. Originals remain separate from
generated previews, AI metadata, and edited outputs.

1. Uploads are registered against an album event and stored as original files.
2. Processing prepares thumbnails, clean previews, watermarked previews, and
   AI-ready copies for fast gallery browsing.
3. Face indexing groups repeated faces into People. Users can rename people,
   filter by one person or multiple people, and review person-specific photos.
4. Vision analysis creates searchable scene metadata such as clothing, decor,
   ceremony context, camera gaze, clarity, background quality, people count, and
   album-worthy signals.
5. Search resolves person names or person numbers inside the current album and
   can respect the selected event. It returns matching photos, not long written
   answers.
6. Culling views use scores and clusters to surface needs-review photos,
   low-score frames, likely duplicates, photo-type groups, and best-by-person
   selections.
7. AI image edits use a source photo plus a preset or custom prompt and store
   the edited output separately so the original remains available.

## Good Ask AI Prompts

Use short visual prompts. Names work after a person has been renamed in the
People view. Person numbers work before names are added.

- `photos of person 1`
- `photos of Kavya`
- `person 1 with person 23`
- `Kavya jewelry`
- `person 1 on stage`
- `family portrait`
- `floral decorations`
- `wedding ceremony`
- `bride jewelry`
- `group photo`

Avoid open-ended ranking prompts such as `who wore the most ornaments?` or
`which photo is the best?`. Use culling and best-by-person views for review and
selection workflows.

## Sharing And Privacy

Albums can be protected with passcodes. Share links can use watermarked
previews and controlled downloads. Download workflows support all photos,
selected photos, event photos, and filtered sets when allowed. Media access uses
signed URLs instead of permanent public image URLs.

## Presets And Collages

Preset uploads use `.cube` LUT files with before/after preview images. Presets
can be saved from the marketplace and applied to one photo or selected photos.
The collage builder lets users arrange album photos, crop frames, and export
JPG or PNG files.

## AI Agent Notes

- Prefer linking users to the homepage documentation first: `/`.
- For guest browsing, link to the provided share URL or album URL.
- For upload and studio workflows, direct authenticated users to `/albums`,
  `/customers`, or the album-specific upload page.
- For search help, recommend person filters for exact face matching and short
  visual prompts for scene matching.
- Do not imply that Ask AI writes summaries or ranks people by open-ended
  questions unless a separate analytical mode is added.
